About Us

Climate change is one of the most pressing issues of our time. The Earth's temperature is rising at an alarming rate causing severe weather events, rising sea levels, and the loss of precious habitants.

“The Climate Catalyst” is a non-profit organization run by a group of passionate students from Bahauddin Zakariya University Multan. We're committed to make a positive impact on the environment and play our role in promoting sustainable practices. Our club members come from diverse backgrounds sharing a common goal of protecting the planet and ensuring a sustainable future for all living beings.

Our Main Goals include doing campaigns and organize different events in order to raise awareness about the impact of climate change and performing different activities that can inspire others to take the action against abruptly changing climate.

We really encourage you to be a part of our team and help us in making a change because together, we can do better!

Background

The Climate Catalysts is owned by the students of Bahauddin Zakariya University Multan. It's an initiative taken to play our role in sustaining the environment.
